Tater Tots Casserole

A handcrafted rendition of the infamous American dish, Tater Tots Casserole

Tater Tots Casserole is a cheesy casserole made with tater tots in the base. This allows it to embody the American dream quite beautifully.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 10.75 oz condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 16 oz frozed tater tots
    • Steps
    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
    2. Cook and stir beef on medium heat until no longer pink.
    3. Stir cream of mushroom soup into beef. Pour mixture into baking dish.
    4. Layer tots evenly over the mixture. Top with cheddar cheese.
    5. Bake until tater tots are golden brown.
