Chicken Tikka Masala

A beautifully curated chicken tikka masala dish.

Chicken tikka masala is a classic indian dish, and is arguably one of the most popular in the States. It consists of roasted, marinated chicken chunks in a deep, often spicy sauce.

Ingredients
  • 1 Cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 4 teaspoons ground cumin, divided
  • 2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp minced fresh ginger
  • 2 tsp salt, divided, or to taste
  • 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts cut into bit-size chunks
  • 4 long skewers
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 8 oz tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped, fresh cilantro
    • Steps
    1. Combine yogurt, lemon juice, 2 teaspoons cumin, cinnamon, cayenne, black pepper, ginger, and 1 teaspoon salt in a large bowl. Stir in chicken, cover, and refrigerate for 1 hour.
    2. Preheat grill on high.
    3. Lightly oil grill, skewer chicken and cook until juices are clear (approx. 5 minutes).
    4. Melt butter in skillet and saute garlic and jalapeno for 1 minute. Season with remaining 2 tsp cumin, paprika, and 1 tsp salt. Stir in tomato sauce and cream. Simmer on low until sauce thinkens (approx. 20 minutes).
    5. And grilled chicken and simmer for an additional 10 minutes. Transfer to serving platter, and garnish with fresh cilantro.
